// Who I Am

Short version: builder first, student second.

I'm a third-year CSE student at IIIT Nagpur, specializing in Data Science and Full Stack Development. I grew up taking things apart to see how they worked, and somewhere along the way that curiosity turned into code. Today I spend most of my time either training models or shipping features — and the best days are when those two things are the same day.

My work sits at the intersection of ML engineering and product. I'm the person who'd rather prototype an end-to-end system over the weekend than spend a month on a deck. Some recent things I've built: a PowerGrid AI forecasting platform used by industry mentors at NTPC, a Walmart Sparkathon platform that reached the national round, and a competitive coding arena used by 500+ students across my campus.

Outside of building, I'm an Algo University Campus Extern, I mentor juniors on DSA, and I help run placement-prep initiatives at IIIT Nagpur. I believe good engineers teach — because the stuff you can explain clearly is the stuff you actually understand.
